PhoneSober Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026
Applicable App: PhoneSober (iOS / Android)

1. Overview

PhoneSober ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data. The App does not collect or upload any personal data to our servers. All data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Data We Process

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide focus reminders, statistics, and personalization features:

Data TypePurposeStorage Location
Focus mode status, daily/total focus duration, reminder play count, history statsFocus tracking and statisticsSecure local device storage
Focus theme data (theme name, color, cumulative duration by theme)Theme-based statistics and chartsLocal device storage
Focus note records (date, theme, text content)Calendar, whiteboard, topic grouping, and searchLocal device storage
Uploaded reminder audio files (including local path/copy)Play personalized remindersLocal device storage
Uploaded meditation video files (including local path/copy)Meditation playback and list managementLocal device storage
Custom lock-screen message, wallpaper image, text style and position settingsPersonalized lock-screen displayLocal device storage
Selected apps to remindWork with system features to trigger remindersLocal device storage
In-app language preferenceUI language displayLocal device storage

The above data is stored only on your device. We cannot access it and do not upload it to any third party or our servers.

3. Permissions

To provide core functionality, the App may request the following system permissions. None are enabled without your consent:

  • Notifications: Used to send reminders while focus mode is active.
  • Photos/Media Library: Used when you select a wallpaper image or upload reminder audio. We only read files you explicitly choose and do not scan or upload your photo library.
  • File Access: Used to import reminder audio or meditation videos from local files/iCloud. We only access files you explicitly select.
  • Screen Time (iOS only): Used to trigger reminders when selected apps are opened. Only used after authorization. We do not collect or upload your app usage records.

You can disable these permissions at any time in system settings. Related features may become unavailable, but other features will continue to work.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.
  • App data does not pass through our servers, so we do not collect or store your content.
  • If you share/open content via other apps or system features (such as Shortcuts), those actions are governed by the privacy policies of those apps/services.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data remains on your device and is retained based on your usage.
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data (including stats, themes, notes, audio, meditation videos, wallpapers, and settings). We keep no server-side copies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not target children under 13 and does not actively collect personal information. We do not identify users through local data and do not upload local data to servers.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published in the App or on this page. If a change affects how locally stored data is handled, we will provide clear notice in the App or on this page.
