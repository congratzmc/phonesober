PhoneSober Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026
Applicable App: PhoneSober (iOS / Android)

The App does not collect or upload any personal data to our servers. All data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Overview

PhoneSober ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data. We believe your focus data and reflections are private and should remain entirely under your control.

2. Data We Process

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide focus reminders, statistics, and personalization features:

Data TypePurposeStorage Location
Focus duration, play count, history statsFocus tracking and statisticsSecure local storage
Theme data & statisticsVisual chartsLocal storage
Focus notes & recordsCalendar & WhiteboardLocal storage
Uploaded audio & video filesPersonalized playbackLocal storage
Lock-screen settings & wallpaperUI PersonalizationLocal storage
App whitelist/blacklist settingsSystem-level remindersLocal storage

3. Permissions

To provide core functionality, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminders while focus mode is active.
  • Photos/Media Library: Only used when you explicitly select a wallpaper or audio. We do not scan your library.
  • Screen Time (iOS): To detect when selected apps are opened. We do not collect or upload usage records.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. Because we keep no server-side copies, once the app is deleted, the data cannot be recovered by us.
